Often Asked Questions

Who are the developers?

As it stands, Nazastech is a proud company of two. From start to finish you'll work with one point of contact, which means you can get the job done without stumbling through gobs of red tape.

How much does a website cost?

This is tough to ballpark– as a website varies based on the scope of work. A one page design → dev project will be about $100. Submit a budget with your request, and we'll let you know if I can work with it. Alternatively, you can contract us hourly.

How long will it take to build a website?

The standard timeframe is a minimum of 7-14 working days (2 weeks) to build your professional website. However, this can be longer depending on your requirements as we do not compromise on the quality of our work while meeting your needs.

You don't design?

The short answer is no. we'll stick to what we do best and let you work with those who do design best.

Do you recommend any designers?

Yes and I'm happy to connect you via email or Twitter. Alternatively, if you're a designer looking for a Webflow development partner, my DM's are open!

How do I know Nazastech can help me?

You don't, and most often, neither do we. At least until we get some of your project details, the more the better. If we can't help you, or need to bring in additional help, we'll share that in your estimate– 100% obligation free.
